Custom apparel

Team gear without the guesswork.

Streamline helps choose garments, decorate the right locations, proof the art, and produce organized apparel runs for schools, clubs, businesses, and community groups.

  • Shirts, hoodies, hats, jackets, uniforms, and team packs
  • Screen print, DTF, heat transfer, names, numbers, and mixed methods
  • Sorted pickup, shipping, or team distribution support

Stickers and decals

Fast decals with a simple quick-buy path.

Customers can upload polished art or rough files. The shop checks the file, confirms size and finish, and moves approved sticker orders into print and cutting.

  • Die-cut stickers, labels, window decals, and vehicle graphics
  • Gloss, matte, outdoor laminate, and sheet label options
  • Local pickup or shipping available from the quick sticker form

Signs and banners

Large-format pieces for events, fields, storefronts, and sponsors.

Wide-format production covers visual pieces that need to read clearly from a distance and survive real-world handling.

  • Banners, yard signs, sponsor boards, posters, wall graphics, and decals
  • Artwork scaling checks and material recommendations
  • Production finishing for pickup, install handoff, or delivery

Embroidery

Stitched apparel that feels polished and durable.

Embroidery is best for professional staff apparel, hats, polos, jackets, bags, and premium spirit wear that needs a more elevated finish.

  • Logo digitizing and placement guidance
  • Thread color matching and proof review
  • Small business apparel, team gear, and school staff pieces

Stores and fulfillment

Cleaner ordering for families, teams, and seasonal groups.

The store directory keeps current shops easy to find, while Streamline handles production, packing, pickup, and distribution details behind the scenes.

  • Schools, clubs, leagues, businesses, fundraisers, and seasonal stores
  • Organized production, packing, pickup, and distribution support
  • Clear paths for existing stores and new custom order requests

Design and proofing

Better files before anything hits production.

Not every customer arrives with perfect artwork. The proofing step helps catch file issues, color concerns, placement problems, and sizing before production starts.

  • Artwork review, mockups, proofing, and basic cleanup
  • Production method guidance based on material and use case
  • Human review before complex or time-sensitive jobs move forward

About

Local people. Real production.

Streamline Digital Imaging is a family-owned custom printing and graphics shop serving Snohomish County teams, schools, businesses, and community groups.

The shop pairs the friendliness of a local counter with the production discipline customers expect from a professional print partner: clear proofs, honest timelines, and finished work that looks ready for game day, event day, or everyday business use.
